`date`
1)	displays the system date and time
2)	print the time in different formats
%D – Display date as mm/dd/yy
%T – Display date as HH:MM:SS
%A – Full weekday name (e.g., Friday)
…
3)	sets the system date and time 
4)	calculate future and past dates
date --date="2 year ago"
date --date="next monday"
