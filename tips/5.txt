REGULAR EXPRESSIONS
gr[ea]y  Match "grey" or "gray".
ab?c    Match "abc" or "ac"; `b' 0 or 1 times.
^Joe    Match name "Joe" only at the beginning.
ab.*c    Match anything between "ab" and character "c".
[0-9]+  Match multiple numbers in a line.
Joe +(Smith|Jones)   
Match First name "Joe" followed by any number of spaces and last name that can be either "Smith" or "Jones".
[a-zA-Z0-9]           [:space:]
